# Design System

Colors:
- Background: #0a0a0a (near black)
- Foreground: #ededed (off white)
- Primary/Accent: #ffffff (pure white for highest contrast)
- Muted: #888888 
- Borders: #333333

Typography:
- Sans: Inter or Geist (system-ui fallback)
- Mono: JetBrains Mono or Geist Mono

Effects:
- Very subtle glassmorphism on floating elements (sidebar, nav)
- Sharp corners or very subtle rounding (2px-4px max)
- High contrast borders

Layout:
- Left sidebar for navigation
- Max width 800px for reading column
- Right sidebar for "Ask the Docs" (AI Elements integration)
